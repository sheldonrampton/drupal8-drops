Books Authored by Sheldon Rampton

Friends In Deed: the Story of US-Nicaragua Sister Cities was my first book, written in collaboration with Liz Chilsen and published by the Wisconsin Coordinating Council on Nicaragua in 1985.

Toxic Sludge Is Good For You: Lies, Damn Lies and the Public Relations Industry (Common Courage Press, 1995) grew out of my work with co-author John Stauber at the Center for Media and Democracy.

Mad Cow USA: Could the Nightmare Happen Here? (Common Courage Press, 1997) was my second collaboration with John.
